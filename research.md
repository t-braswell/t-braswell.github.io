I am currently a member of the[Sethuraman Lab](https://www.sethuramanlab.com/) at SDSU, working under [Arun Sethuraman](https://biology.sdsu.edu/people/arun-sethuraman/) on computational biology and bioinformatics. Currently, I am attempting to refine the methods established by Tamsen Dunn's [specKS](https://github.com/tamsen/specks) simulator for polyploid evolution, by using Gaussian Mixture models rather than naive RMS error reduction for comparing simulation results with empirical data.

I am also doing Thesis reseach with [Dr. Christopher Curtis](https://cwcurtis.github.io/) on machine learning and link prediction on bipartite graphs. this is an extension of Maryam Sabagh's [Master's thesis on the subject](https://sciety.org/articles/activity/10.32388/074o28 ), looking for ways to better capture local graph structure for graph neural networks.

Past research and projects for classes include work on [Converge speeds of optimization algorithms](/downloads/693a_final_paper.pdf), [Polynomial division from a Fourier Perspective](/downloads/668_presentation.pdf )
